Erik's Best Video Games

My favorite video games ever

Mass Effect

One of the best stories I've experienced in a game series. Love the characters and universe that they created.

Skyrim

Endless possibilities of what to do, especially with mods. One of the best open world games.

Tears of the Kingdom

Might be my favorite game of all time, but I still have to finish it. However, this game has so much to do, and I want to do all of it at the same time. Exploration, puzzles, combat, building cool contraptions, it's all amazingly fun.
